The experience

How A11yShip works

A structured, facilitated experience that takes participants through a series of guided challenges — each designed to surface a specific accessibility barrier.

👁️

Visual barriers

Simulated low vision, colour blindness, and contrast challenges reveal how much visual design decisions affect real users.

⌨️

Motor barriers

Keyboard-only challenges reveal the frustration of inaccessible UX design and the importance of focus management.

🧠

Cognitive barriers

Simulated cognitive load tasks demonstrate the impact of complex language, cluttered layouts, and poor error handling.

🔊

Auditory barriers

Audio-dependent content challenges reveal why captions, transcripts, and visual cues are critical for inclusion.
