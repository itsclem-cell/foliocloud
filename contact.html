Book a call

Let's start with a conversation.

The Kickstarter call is 30 minutes, free, and no-obligation. You'll leave with a clear sense of your accessibility priorities and what a practical programme could look like for your organisation.

What to expect

  • A 30-minute video or phone call with an accessibility consultant
  • Discussion of your current situation, obligations, and goals
  • An honest view of where your biggest risks and opportunities lie
  • A clear recommendation on which service tier fits best
  • No hard sell — just practical guidance

Good fit for

  • Public sector organisations with PSBAR obligations
  • Regulated businesses preparing for EAA compliance
  • L&D and HR teams building accessible learning
  • Digital teams planning WCAG audits
  • Organisations post-complaint or post-audit
