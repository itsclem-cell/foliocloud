ING: From reactive auditing to embedded accessibility practice

ING came to us with a familiar challenge: multiple digital products, inconsistent accessibility standards, and growing pressure from the European Accessibility Act deadline.

Over 18 months, we worked alongside their design and engineering teams to audit their core customer-facing products, build a shared component library with accessibility baked in, and deliver capability training across four product squads.

Virgin Money: Kickstarter to full accessibility programme

Starting with a focused Kickstarter engagement, Virgin Money quickly recognised the depth of accessibility debt across their digital products. We supported a full programme of remediation, training, and process change across their web banking platform.

  • Full WCAG 2.2 AA audit of 3 key customer journeys
  • Design system accessibility review and remediation
  • Screen reader testing with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver
  • Accessibility champion programme launched

A11yShip programme

CloserStill Media: Building accessibility culture through A11yShip

CloserStill Media ran our A11yShip experience across their product and technology teams — 45 participants across 3 cohorts. The gamified format made accessibility learning approachable, memorable, and genuinely enjoyable for teams who'd previously found the topic daunting.

  • 45 staff trained across design, development, and content
  • 92% of participants rated experience 4/5 or higher
  • Internal accessibility peer-review process established
  • 3 product squads now run their own a11y reviews

Audit & Statement

QS Awards shortlisted: Accessibility-first event platform

We were brought in to assess the accessibility of a major awards event platform — reviewing the registration flow, nomination portal, and live voting system. Our audit findings informed a round of targeted remediation that resulted in a WCAG 2.2 AA compliant platform and a QS award shortlisting for inclusive design.

  • End-to-end audit of registration and voting flows
  • WCAG 2.2 AA compliant platform achieved
  • Published accessibility statement with enforcement route
  • QS Award shortlisted for Inclusive Design
