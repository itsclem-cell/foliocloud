Our focus areas

Compliance, capability & inclusion.

Three distinct problem areas, addressed together through our service tiers.

⚖️

Risk & Compliance

Support teams to understand WCAG obligations, prioritise issues and show a defensible plan for action. Relevant for organisations subject to the Public Sector Bodies Accessibility Regulations, Equality Act, or European Accessibility Act.

🛠️

Delivery Capability

Move from one-off fixes to sustained good habits across content, learning, product and communications teams. Training, templates and embedded processes that last beyond the engagement.

🤝

Human Inclusion

Make services easier to access for disabled people, neurodivergent users and people experiencing mental health barriers. Grounded in real user needs, not just technical criteria.
